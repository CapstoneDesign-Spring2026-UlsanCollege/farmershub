# Project Title

A Hub for farmers

# Problem Statement

Many farmers struggle to find a platform where they are represented properly. They feel that there isn't a one way solution to their problem for a need for community and a place for their products to be sold in a wide scale.

# Target Users

Who will use this system?

Examples:

- Farmers
- Consumers looking for reliable farming product sources
- Students learning about farming
- Teachers
- Businesses


Your users:

Farmers will use our platflorm to create their profile , pitch their respective businesses and form communities. Likewise, consumers find reliable sources for their purchases. Researchers and students can likewise find valuable inormation. 

# Project Goal

 Farmers promote their business and form communities with other farmers and interested parties. Consumers and others browse through products and farmers that they find reliable.  

# Core Features (First Version)


- A profile for farmers
- Connect with other farmers
- Browse through the products 
- Sell and Purchase 



# Demo Scenario (Very Important)


Your demo scenario:

Step 1: A farmer promotes his/her products/ site
Step 2: A consumer likes a product 
Step 3: Consumer contacts the farmer
Step 4: The product is shipped

# Project Scope Rules

To keep the project realistic:

- Start small
- Add features gradually
- Focus on **working software**

Avoid projects that are:

- too large
- unclear
- impossible to demo

A good project is:

```text
Demoable
Feasible
Well-scoped
```

# What a Good Project Looks Like

Strong projects usually:

- solve a clear problem
- have a simple demo
- grow gradually
- focus on reliability
- maintain clear documentation

The goal is not to build something huge.
