# Project Title

A Hub for farmers

# Problem Statement

Many farmers struggle to find a platform where they are represented properly. They feel that there isn't a one way solution to their problem for a need for community and a place for their products to be sold in a wide scale.

# Target Users

Who will use this system?

Examples:

- Farmers
- Consumers looking for reliable farming product sources
- Students learning about farming


<!-- Niche down. Start with one or two and grow from there. -->

Our users:

Farmers will use our platflorm to create their profile , pitch their respective businesses and form communities. Likewise, consumers find reliable sources for their purchases. Researchers and students can likewise find valuable inormation. 

# Project Goal

 Farmers promote their business and form communities with other farmers and interested parties. Consumers and others browse through products and farmers that they find reliable.
