## 1) Project title
- **FarmerHub**

## 2) One-sentence pitch
Fill this in: Hello guys how would you love having direct reach to your product from farmers!!

> **For customers who need direct access to farmers, this is an app where you can connect with farmers that can provide you details and videos of how farming is done.**

---

## 3) Problem (what pain are we solving?)

- Consumers do not know where their food actually comes from.
- Farmers often have difficulty reaching customers directly.
- Middlemen increase product price and reduce farmer profit.

**Why does it matter?**

- Customers want transparency and trust in food production.
- Farmers should earn fair prices for their products.

---

## 4) Target users

- **Primary user:** Customers who want to buy or learn about farm products directly from farmers.
- **Secondary user (optional):** Farmers who want to showcase their farming process and connect with buyers.

---

## 5) MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

### MVP = the smallest version we can demo end-to-end.

- User registration and login
- Farmers can create a profile and upload farming videos
- Customers can view farmer profiles and videos
- Basic product listing from farmers
- Simple search or browsing of farmers/products

### NOT in MVP (explicitly out of scope)

- Online payment system
- Delivery logistics system
- Advanced recommendation system
- Real-time live streaming

---

## 6) Demo plan (required)

### What will we demo by Week 4?

- Basic UI design of the FarmerHub website
- Simple homepage with farmer listing
- Static farmer profiles

### What will we demo by Midterm (Week 8)?

- User login and registration
- Farmers can upload product details and videos
- Customers can view farmer profiles and products

### What will we demo by Final (Week 16)?

- Fully functional FarmerHub web app
- Farmer profile management
- Video and product browsing
- Deployed working website demo
